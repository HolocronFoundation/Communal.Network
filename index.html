Why Tweed.social?

You are a Person, Not a Product

We don't make our money selling your data. We don't even collect anything beyond the bare necessities needed to actually run our site (e.g. the actual message you send). We work for you, not advertisers.

Global

Tweed.social is the first truly global social network. By leveraging the power of Ethereum, we are able to create a truly global network, not dependent upon the whims of commercial or governmental interests.

Decentralized

Tweed.social runs on the Ethereum network. The Ethereum is composed of well over 10,000 computers, located around the world. The network is approximately distributed as follows:

  • 42.5% - United States
  • 12.5% - China
  • 7.5% - Canada
  • 3.5% - Germany
  • 3.5% - Russia
  • 3.0% - United Kingdom
  • .
  • .
  • .

Secure

We don't store your passwords. You are in charge of your own security. We don't have any servers to hack.

Forever

As long as the Ethereum Network runs, Tweed.social will too.

Censor-resistant

The peer to peer nature of the Ethereum network makes it extremely difficult (compared to traditional sites) to block access.

We also can't censor you via algorithms. All our algorithms which actually pull our data and display it run in your own browser. You can even take a look yourself!

Spam-resistant

Unlike traditional social networks, where there is no inherent spam deterent, the minute cost is a spam deterent. Limited spammers, limited election interference. More of the content you actually want to see.
